📊 Financial Vision & Token Flow

How Narrrf's World powers growth while rewarding its true heroes — the community.

🌍 Our Financial Ethos

Narrrf's World is built to reward participation — not extract value. 80% of all capital generated through NFT mints is reinvested directly into the community through Benefit Seasons, rewards, trait perks, and ecosystem bonuses.

🧮 Capital Allocation Breakdown

  • 80% — Community Rewards (Benefit Showdowns, raffles, wheel spins, staking bonuses)
  • 15% — Marketing & Growth (Web3 forums, collab deals, exchange listing, Hytopia dev)
  • 10% — Operations & Dev (Server costs, bots, platform tools, API services)
  • 5% — Support Crew (moderators, tech support, security layers)

🔐 Founder Transparency

The founder of Narrrf's World takes no salary. All time, art direction, coding, and partnership building is community-driven. The only returns are earned through organic ownership (NFTs, staking, and trait advantages). Server utilities and small essentials are covered under the operations pool.

🔄 Revenue Streams (External)

  • Royalties from secondary NFT sales
  • $SPOINC utility and swap services
  • Real-world partner integrations in Hytopia World
  • Limited edition merch & licensing (non-core revenue)
