Privacy Policy & Legal Notice

Last Updated: January 28, 2025

Welcome to Narrrf's World ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with our Web3 applications.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Create an account or profile
  • Connect your Discord account
  • Connect your cryptocurrency wallet
  • Participate in games or activities
  • Contact us for support
  • Subscribe to our newsletter

2. Discord Integration Data

When you connect your Discord account, we may collect:

  • Discord username and display name
  • Discord user ID
  • Profile picture/avatar
  • Server membership information
  • Role assignments and permissions

3. Wallet and NFT Information

When you connect your cryptocurrency wallet, we may collect:

  • Wallet addresses (public keys only)
  • NFT ownership verification data
  • Transaction history related to our services
  • Collection membership information

4. Game and Activity Data

We collect information about your interactions with our services:

  • Game scores and achievements
  • DSPOINC token balances and transactions
  • User preferences and settings
  • Communication and chat logs

5. Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information:

  • IP address and location data
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information and operating system
  • Usage patterns and analytics
  • Error logs and performance data

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

1. Service Provision

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Process transactions and manage accounts
  • Enable Discord role management
  • Verify NFT ownership and grant access
  • Facilitate game functionality and scoring

2. User Experience

  • Personalize content and recommendations
  • Improve our services and user interface
  • Provide customer support and assistance
  • Send important updates and notifications

3. Security and Compliance

  • Protect against fraud and abuse
  • Ensure compliance with legal obligations
  • Maintain security of our systems
  • Investigate and resolve disputes

4. Analytics and Research

  • Analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Conduct research and development
  • Improve service performance
  • Develop new features and services

Cookie Policy

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us provide and improve our services.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled. They include:

  • Authentication Cookies: Maintain your login session and security
  • Session Cookies: Remember your preferences during your visit
  • Security Cookies: Protect against fraud and ensure secure connections

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization:

  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and choices
  • Language Cookies: Store your language preference
  • Feature Cookies: Enable advanced features and interactions

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website:

  • Performance Cookies: Monitor website performance and speed
  • Usage Cookies: Track page views and user behavior
  • Error Cookies: Identify and fix technical issues

Cookie Consent

We respect your privacy and provide you with control over non-essential cookies. You can:

  • Accept all cookies for full functionality
  • Decline non-essential cookies while maintaining core features
  • Modify your cookie preferences at any time

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except in the following circumstances:

1. Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in:

  • Website hosting and maintenance
  • Database management and security
  • Payment processing and transactions
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Customer support and communication

2. Discord Integration

When you use Discord features, we may share:

  • User verification status with Discord servers
  • Role assignment information for community management
  • Activity data for community engagement

3. Blockchain and Web3 Services

For NFT verification and blockchain interactions:

  • Wallet addresses for transaction verification
  • NFT ownership data for role assignment
  • Collection membership for access control

4. Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Respond to legal requests and court orders

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Security Measures

  • Encryption: Data transmission and storage encryption
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and monitoring
  • Incident Response: Procedures for security breaches
  • Employee Training: Security awareness and best practices

Important Security Note

While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights regarding your personal information:

1. Access and Control

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format

2. Communication Preferences

  • Marketing: Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Notifications: Control notification settings
  • Newsletters: Manage subscription preferences

3. Cookie Management

  • Browser Settings: Control cookies through browser preferences
  • Third-Party Tools: Use privacy tools and extensions
  • Opt-Out: Decline non-essential cookies

4. Account Management

  • Profile Updates: Modify your account information
  • Connection Management: Control Discord and wallet connections
  • Data Export: Download your personal data

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Retention Periods

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Game Data: Kept for historical records and analytics
  • Transaction Data: Retained for financial and legal compliance
  • Communication Data: Kept for customer service purposes

Data Deletion

When you request account deletion, we will:

  • Remove your personal information from our systems
  • Delete associated game data and preferences
  • Remove Discord and wallet connections
  • Retain only necessary data for legal compliance

International Data Transfers

Your personal information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information during these transfers.

Safeguards

  • Standard Contractual Clauses: EU-approved data transfer agreements
  • Adequacy Decisions: Transfer to countries with adequate protection
  • Certification Programs: Industry-recognized privacy frameworks
  • Technical Measures: Encryption and access controls
